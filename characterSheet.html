Welcome to Vampires RPG

The online roll-playing game

1. Fill out your character's name and enter your name.
2. Select a character type, then class.
3. Click the Play Now button to play.
4. Click the Attack button to attack, then the Defend button
for more realistic combat. 5. Watch your experience and level increase with each fallen foe. 6. If you should perish in battle, refresh the browser to play again.
7. If this makes sense, click the Got It button.
